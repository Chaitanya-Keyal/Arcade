Monopoly Documentation

Format of messages:
Send: (args*)
Recv: (Sender's UUID, args*)

Roll:
    (ROLL, (Dice1Roll,Dice2Roll))

Buy:
    (BUY, PropertyNumber)

Build/ Sell:
    (BUILD, PropertyNumber, NumberofHouses, bool: Sell)

Mortgage/ Unmortgage:
    (MORTGAGE, bool: mortgage, [Property1, Property2, ...])

End Turn:
    (END)
