Welcome to the Arcade!
You can play two games here: Chess and Monopoly

-   You can create your own room or join a room.
-   Public Rooms can be joined by selecting the room from the room list.
-   Private Rooms can be joined by entering the room id which is to be shared by the host.

Under the tab on top right corner, you can:
    -   Switch between Dark and Light mode
    -   Change your Password
    -   Edit your Profile Picture
    -   Choose a default game tab to open at startup

User data:
    -   Stats
    -   Leaderboard

Everything is pretty straightforward and simple. If you have any queries or ideas, drop them here. Have fun!
